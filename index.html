Explanation of drought levels
For each county the land is divided in five categories and each category gets assigned the percentage of land that follows in this category. Below is a table with the different categories. The combined drought severity is calculated as follows: 0.2 * D0 + 0.4 * D1 + 0.6 * D2 + 0.8 * D3 + 1.0 * D4.
  • None: no drought
  • D0: abnormally dry
  • D1: moderate drought
  • D2: severe drought category
  • D3: extreme drought
  • D4: exceptional drought
